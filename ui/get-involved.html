Get Involved

Financial Support

Operation Meraki thrives on generous financial support to fuel vital initiatives – from supplying essentials to strategic advertising for reaching more veterans in need. We're committed to transparency and annually report spending to meet nonprofit standards. Join us in making a lasting impact!

Donate Now

Board of Directors

Passionate about supporting transitioning Veterans? Join our Board of Directors. We seek individuals with expertise in nonprofit leadership, accounting, legal systems, policy, ecology, or climate advocacy. Apply or share this opportunity to create positive change for veterans and their families.

Apply Now

Donate Camera Equipment

Do you want to put your old photo gear to good use? We are in need of all kinds of working equipment in good condition for our program. We’ll use your pre-owned gear to equip our classes and teach Veterans across the nation about the wonders of photography.

Apply Now

Become a Volunteer

We enjoy the “ah-ha” moments, the shared stories, the excitement of a new job, and creating new therapies that improve life for Veterans. We build strong connections with our Veterans work hard to get them moving in the right direction. If this sounds like you, please volunteer today.
