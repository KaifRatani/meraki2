I am Veterans

You’ve worn the uniform, faced challenges most will never know, and carried a weight that deserves more than a simple thank you. At Operation Meraki, we offer more than gratitude — we offer community, connection, and resources to help you move forward with dignity, pride, and strength.

I am Volunteers

Volunteering with Operation Meraki means becoming a bridge between those who served and the life they’re striving to build. Your commitment helps veterans feel seen, supported, and empowered — one conversation, one action, one connection at a time.

I am Donors

Every contribution helps us provide life-changing services to veterans, offering them the support, resources, and community they need to succeed.

From mental health support and career training to reintegration programs, your generosity enables our heroes to thrive in their next chapter.

Our Mission

We challenge and change the veteran transition landscape by helping our service members and their families bridge from the uniform to building new professional and personal lives.

Our Vision

Our vision is to address the post-military challenges faced by Veterans. Operation Meraki is dedicated to reducing underemployment among veterans and redirecting their emotional and cognitive capabilities towards a successful transition.
