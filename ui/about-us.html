About Us

Operation Meraki is a non-profit organization founded in June 2020 to guide and support veterans through their transition process of returning home after active duty with a variety of programs.

In the spirit of the Greek word "Meraki," meaning to infuse soul, creativity, or love into what you do, our organization is dedicated to approaching this mission with utmost dedication and care.

Our Founder
Operation Meraki was founded by Michael Thomas, a 9/11 First Responder and disabled combat Veteran, who personally faced the challenges of transitioning from the military. Inspired by his own struggles with anxiety, depression, undiagnosed PTSD, and suicidal thoughts, Michael created Operation Meraki to offer support, understanding, and a community for veterans to ensure that each finds strength, companionship, and support to overcome the hurdles of transition and start a fulfilling new chapter in life.

Our Mission
We help our service members and their families transition from the uniform to new professional and personal lives by changing the veteran transition landscape.
Our Vision
Our vision is to tackle post-military challenges by reducing underemployment and facilitating a successful transition by harnessing their emotional and cognitive abilities.
Community Engagement
Transitioning can be a solitary journey, and we recognize the importance of a robust support system. Operation Meraki actively facilitates community engagement through social events, networking opportunities, and mentorship programs. Our goal is to connect veterans with like-minded individuals, providing a sense of belonging that combats the isolation often experienced during this period. We believe that by fostering relationships and building a supportive community, veterans can navigate their journey with strength and resilience.

Our Unique Approach

Born during the challenging times of the pandemic, Operation Meraki addresses gaps in veteran transition. Our tailored programs and the Scout to Pathfinder process provide personalized support, empowering veterans to confidently embrace their next mission.

We're not just an organization; we're a community committed to supporting veterans at every step. Join us on this transformative journey, where dedicated souls meet impactful service.
