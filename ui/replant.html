Replant
Operation Meraki RePlant is a Sustainable Food and Farming Program featuring indoor horizontal agricultural gardens in community spaces. Veterans and community members come together weekly to cultivate vegetables, fostering healing through shared activities and conversations. RePlant offers workshops and workdays to provide Veterans with urban gardening experience, aiming to increase food production sustainably and reduce environmental impact.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I bring a friend or family member?
Yes, family members are encouraged to join our programs as part of the healing process.
Do I have to pay anything?
No, all Operation Meraki programs are completely free for veterans and their families.
Do I need to bring any supplies?
No, all necessary equipment and supplies will be provided for the program.
What if I have no experience?
Our programs are designed for all experience levels, from beginners to advanced. No prior experience is necessary.
